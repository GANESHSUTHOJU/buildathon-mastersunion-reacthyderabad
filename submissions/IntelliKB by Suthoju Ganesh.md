## Project Description

IntelliKB is an AI-powered workplace knowledge management platform designed to centralize organizational knowledge and make information instantly accessible through natural language conversations.

Organizations often store important information across multiple sources such as PDFs, DOCX files, employee handbooks, onboarding guides, internal policies, URLs, and Slack conversations. Employees spend significant time searching for information or repeatedly asking colleagues for answers.

IntelliKB solves this challenge by providing a centralized knowledge platform where users can upload documents, organize information into knowledge spaces, generate AI-powered summaries, and retrieve accurate answers with source citations through a conversational interface.

The platform is designed for enterprises, teams, HR departments, and developers who need fast access to organizational knowledge while maintaining proper access control and knowledge governance.

---

## Approach

We approached the problem by focusing on how employees actually search for information in a workplace environment.

Instead of building a simple chatbot, we designed a complete AI-powered knowledge platform that combines document management, semantic search, AI-generated summaries, access control, analytics, and Slack-based interactions.

Our solution allows organizations to upload and organize documents into Personal, Team, and Organization knowledge spaces. The AI assistant retrieves relevant information from the uploaded knowledge base and provides grounded responses with citations and confidence scores.

To improve usability and innovation, we implemented features such as an Interactive Knowledge Graph, Knowledge Health Score, AI Auto-Tagging, Semantic Search Explorer, Analytics Dashboard, and Slack Experience Center.

This creates a complete enterprise-grade knowledge management ecosystem rather than just a document question-answering system.
